Minecraft Terrain Generator

3D Graphics Programming assignment using C++ and DirectX 12 to create a Minecraft style terrain generator. This was a second year group project headed by myself in which I took responsibility for most of the coding including terrain generation. The university module focused on providing us with an understanding of the core principles and techniques that underpin computer graphics and also develop our programming skills by introducing us to industry tools and techniques employed in the construction of rendering systems for computer graphics.
