One of my favourite parts of Halo Reach when I played it as a teenager was the forge mode that allowed players to create their own custom maps and game modes inside the game but I came to realise that other players in the community were using it in a different way. Part of Halo Reach was being able to share your creations with the community through the fileshare system, and you could see the most popular maps and game modes that people have created. You could also see the most popular screenshots that people had taken as well and these didn't look anything like the typical screenshot, they were very artistic. I realised that people had been using the theatre mode combined with forge mode to create interesting effects and filters for beautiful screenshots and I got really into this, so much so that I started to enter my screenshots into the Bungie screenshot competitions! Below are some of the screenshots I took and hosted on my fileshare :)
